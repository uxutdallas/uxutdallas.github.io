About this event

The world has drastically changed since the COVID-19 pandemic began. In our rapidly changing world, what lessons can we take away to create improved and meaningful design?

Join us for an in-person UX Conference where you will have the opportunity to attend talks from various UX industry professionals. It is an excellent opportunity to network with others in the UX community and expand your UX knowledge.
