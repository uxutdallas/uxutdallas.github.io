Wireframing 101

Wednesday, November 7 | 7 - 9 pm | ATC 2.918

Learn the basics of wireframing - an essential step in the UX design process.

Join us for a workshop where we'll talk about wireframing - the laying out of apps & websites - including how to do it and why it's important. Practice by redesigning an app or website that you already know, like Facebook or Snapchat!
