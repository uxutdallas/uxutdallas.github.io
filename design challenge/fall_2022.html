Banquet

Join us for the 2022 Design Challenge Banquet!

On November 16th from 6:00 -8:00 PM we will be hosting a banquet at the Alumni Center to celebrate design and commemorate our 2022 Design Challenge participants.

This will feature a networking mixer with industry professionals, esteemed UTD faculty and your peers! Dinner will be served, and we will conclude the night by awarding the top contestants!

Design challange participants that have submitted a project should use the RSVP sent to their email to confirm their spot!


Those interested in attending should use this General RSVP to join the waitlist. Invites will be on a first come first serve basis, so please check your email near the banquet date to see if you were invited!
